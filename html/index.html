    What is Aqarchain?

    Aqarchain is a blockchain (tezos) powered real estate investing platform whose aim is to create a decentralized ecosystem where investment in real estate is as easy as investing in shares on the stock market.

    This aim is achieved by fractionalizing the real estate properties.

    Fractional Ownership

    A real estate property is the asset that is divided for fractional ownership. The Property is divided into sq cm and each sq cm is One share in the property. Investors invest into the property by buying multiple shares of the property.

    About Aqarchain

    Aqar chain is 100%-private owned subsidiary of Smart Chain Information Technology Consultancy Ltd. It is proposed to be incorporated in the Dubai International Financial Centre (“DIFC”) under the proposed name Aqar Chain Limited. The main objective of the company is operating a Crowd funding platform.

    Why DIFC?

    The Applicant Firm believes that DIFC is the ideal location to base its business given the regulatory environment, geographical location providing proximity to target clients as well as DIFC excellent business support services. DIFC has more than 15 years of history and more than 500 financial institutions. The robust DFSA regulatory regime has proven itself to be respected and valued by investors. Tested court system and widely recognized as the most mature common law court system in the Middle East with a world class legal and regulatory framework adds value. DIFC facilities are outstanding and impressive, and overall clients feel comfortable knowing their financial services provider is regulated by the DFSA and based in DIFC.

    Aqarchain Tokens Ecoystem

    AQRX Token

    Premined Utility token on the Tezos FA2.2 Protocol are used as a fund raising instrument and is not in any manner a part of the ecosystem

    The AQRX tokens after public sale will be listed on exchanges.

    AQRX Token

    AQR Token is a stable coin pegged to USD and used for transactions in the Aqarchain ecosystem. It is a system tool to represent client money. These tokens are not premined and are governed by a smartcontract with a Mint and burn functionality

    Security Token

    Asset backed security tokens are issued on the Aqar chain ecosystem for each property separately and have an intrinsic value that is derived from the Property value. Each sq cm is one security token of the Asset

    The Aqarchain platform has
    3 types of users

    Aqar chain Admin

    This user is the owner of the property or an agent authorized with the transaction regarding this property. This user is also the seller of the property

    Retail Investor

    This user is the user who will buy properties on the platform, This user deposits money into the platform and invests into the tokenized properties.

    Platform Admin

    These users are the operations and management team of Aqar Chain who will be responsible for daily operations, approvals, KYC, diligence and reports.

    Platform Features

    Fractional Ownership

    By dividing the property in divisible shares of each sq cm allows investors to invest in minimum value and spread the risk. Investors always have an option to increase or decrease their share percentage in the Asset. This enables even smaller investors get to invest in what is usually reserved for the larger corporates and institutions.

    Crowd Funding

    An age old tradition that has been commercialised in this era with the advent of technology. People have been pooling money to buy assets since ages and it has always been in closed circles of family and friends, now it has become more easier and efficient. We ensure that every ones interest is secured and safe.

    Technology

    Tezos is an open-source platform for assets and applications backed by a global community of validators, researchers, and builders. Tezos addresses key barriers facing blockchain adoption to date: smart contract safety, long-term upgradability, and open participation.Tezos is designed to provide the safety and code correctness required for assets and other high value use cases.
